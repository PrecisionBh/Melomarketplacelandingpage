Melo Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Melo ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Melo mobile application and website. This page informs users regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when using our Service.

Information We Collect

When using Melo we may collect the following information:

  • Email address
  • User account information
  • Profile information
  • Listings and uploaded images
  • Messages between users
  • Transaction data related to purchases and sales

Payments

Payments on Melo are processed securely through third-party payment providers such as Stripe. Melo does not store full credit card information.

How We Use Information

The collected data is used to:

  • Provide and maintain the marketplace
  • Enable secure transactions
  • Allow communication between buyers and sellers
  • Improve platform functionality
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information using secure technologies and encryption where appropriate.

Third-Party Services

Melo may use trusted third-party services including payment processors and hosting providers to operate the platform.

User Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their account and associated data by contacting support.
