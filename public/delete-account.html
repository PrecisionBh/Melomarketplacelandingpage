Request Account Deletion

If you would like to delete your Melo account and associated data, please contact our support team.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email from the address associated with your Melo account to:

support@melomarketplace.app

Include the subject line:

"Account Deletion Request"

Our team will process the request and permanently remove your account and associated data within a reasonable timeframe.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • User profile information
  • Account credentials
  • Listings created by the user
  • Messages and user generated content

Data That May Be Retained

Some transaction records may be retained if required for legal, tax, or fraud prevention purposes.
